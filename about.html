I was introduced to programming at university, when I was making audio effects processors or algorithmic music generators. Back then I was using an obscure high level graphical language called Pure Data. Two things immediately caught my interest about programming. The first is the creative scope of possibilities - something which will always greatly excite me. The second is the challenge of it, that rewarding feeling you get work out a more elegant solution to a problem or a solution to that bug in your code.

Since then I have been teaching myself C++, JavaScript, HTML5 and CSS3. This website has been created to document my journey and also to act as a portfolio.

In my spare time I create what can loosely be called music using a variety of programs. I am also interested in psychology, stand up comedy and anything to do with Space.
